🏠📊 Boston Housing Market & AI Predictive Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project delivers a comprehensive analysis of the Boston Housing Market using Power BI, integrating advanced AI-driven insights to uncover the key socio-economic and environmental factors influencing property values.

Rather than focusing solely on visualization, the report leverages AI features to generate intelligent, data-backed conclusions.

📊 Key Dashboards

The report consists of three fully interactive pages:

1️⃣ Market Overview & Value Analysis 💰

🔹 Average Market Price
Displays the overall mean property value ($23.75K).

🔹 Property Distribution
Categorizes housing inventory into:

Luxury

Mid-Range

Budget

🔹 Price Trends Analysis
Examines how:

Property age

Highway accessibility (RAD)
impact overall market value.

2️⃣ Neighborhood Safety & Socio-Economic Analysis 🛡️

🔹 Crime Risk Index
Monitors regional safety levels (Average: 1.42).

🔹 Environmental Impact
Tracks Nitrogen Oxide (NOX) levels and pollution patterns relative to distance from employment centers.

🔹 Economic Status Correlation
Analyzes the relationship between property prices and lower-income population percentage.

3️⃣ AI-Powered Market Insights 🤖

🔹 Key Influencers Visual
Identifies that the number of rooms (RM) is the strongest driver of price increases.

🔹 Decomposition Tree
Enables deep-dive analysis to understand how different features contribute to total average value.

🔹 Smart Narrative
Automatically generates text-based insights summarizing critical trends.
